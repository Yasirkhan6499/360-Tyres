    Our Process

    Stock Arrives

    When stock arrives in our UK warehouse, it is visually inspected by professional specialists in a facility equipped with bright LED lighting.

    Pressure Testing

    The stock is then pressure tested, which entails calculating the amount of air pushed into the inner lining of your tyre in pounds per square inch (PSI) or bar pressure to ensure there are no leaks.

    Detailing

    The tyre stock is then meticulously detailed in preparation for photography and packaging.

    Dispatch

    Stock is then sent out via our partner logistical firm via a signed-for way, with all orders placed before 1pm Monday through Friday being shipped the same day.
